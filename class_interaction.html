The basic Interaction class, used to represent a potential function.

Specific interactions should derive from this.

virtual flt Interaction::pressure ( Box &  box)
pure virtual

Partial pressure due to this Interaction.

\(P = \sum_{\left<i,j \right>} \vec r_{ij} \cdot \vec F_{ij}\), or equivalently \(P = \sum_i \vec r_i \cdot \vec F_i\) Note that the full pressure involves all interactions and temperature
